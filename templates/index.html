How to use Movie Founder

Find your favorite movies quickly and easily!

  • Type the movie title, genre, actor, or year of release in the appropriate fields.
  • Use autocomplete: start typing and suggestions will appear.
  • Click "Search" or press Enter to find movies.
  • Click popular searches on the left to repeat popular searches.
  • Use the "Back" and "Next" buttons to navigate through the results pages.
